ABOUT FIT

Future Institute of Technology (FIT) is approved by AICTE and affiliated with MAKAUT. FIT offers undergraduate programmes in a wide range of engineering disciplines. Numerous of its students have been hired by highly prestigious multinational corporations. Over the years, FIT has solidified its position as a top institution in West Bengal, providing students with an excellent foundation to launch successful careers as engineers.

ABOUT CulAKriti

CulAkriti is the official cultural club of FIT. CulAkriti's motto is to cultivate a sense of camaraderie among the students of the institute and instil a sense of unity among the students. It encourages all students to participate in different socio-cultural activities to broaden their horizons of experience outside of the classroom in order to help them realise their full potential. CulAkriti is proud to present UNICORN'23.

ABOUT UNICORN

UNICORN is the flagship cultural fest of FIT. Since 2016, UNICORN has had consistent success in providing students from across the city with a top-notch platform to display their talents in a range of categories, including singing, dancing, and fashion shows, to name a few. Shilajit Majumdar, Fakira, Pata R Maruddyan, Prithibi, Underground Authority, The Milliputs, and Ehsaas India, among other well-known artists, have helped to make UNICORN a star-studded event each year. We are happy to be returning with UNICORN'23 this year.
